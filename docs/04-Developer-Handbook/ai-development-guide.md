# AI Development Guide

## Purpose

Define how AI-assisted engineering work should be used in this repository.

## AI-Assisted Development Philosophy

- AI is a delivery accelerator, not the owner of product truth.
- AI should reduce mechanical effort, not weaken review discipline.
- AI output is useful only when scoped, validated, and documented.

## Repository AI Policy

- AI can assist with planning, implementation, review, testing strategy, and
  documentation.
- AI must not fabricate validation results.
- AI must not claim external provider behavior that the repository does not
  implement.
- AI output never replaces human approval for merge or release.

## Responsibilities

- Sol shapes the task, continuity, and boundaries.
- Codex executes the approved slice and reports actual outcomes.
- Human owners remain accountable for final decisions.

## Cost-Saving Workflow

- Reuse handbook, tracker, and task docs before expanding context.
- Keep prompts bounded to one approved slice.
- Prefer targeted repository inspection over broad exploratory runs.
- Avoid redundant validation that does not change decision quality.

## Advisory Use

- AI suggestions are advisory.
- Human reviewers decide whether the change is correct, safe, and complete.
